What is Physics?

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand how the universe works at every scale [web:1]. From the quantum realm of subatomic particles to the cosmic expanse of galaxies, physics provides the mathematical and conceptual framework for comprehending matter, energy, space, and time.

It serves as the foundation upon which all natural sciences are built, bridging theoretical knowledge with practical applications that define our modern technological civilization [web:7]. Physics not only explains natural phenomena but also enables us to predict, control, and harness the forces of nature.
